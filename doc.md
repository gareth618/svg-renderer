# SVG renderer

## `svg`

- `viewBox`: `minX minY width height`

## `line`

- `x1`
- `y1`
- `x2`
- `x2`

## `rect`

- `x` top-left corner x
- `y` top-left corner y
- `rx` x radius **(opt)**
- `ry` y radius **(opt)**
- `width`
- `height`

## `circle`

- `cx` center x
- `cy` center y
- `r` radius

## `ellipse`

- `cx` center x
- `cy` center y
- `rx` x radius
- `ry` y radius

## `polyline`

- `points`: `x1,y1 x2,y2 ...`

## presentation attributes

- `color` hex, rgb, keyword
- `fill` color
- `opacity` număr între `0` și `1`
- `stroke` color
- `stroke-width` număr `px`
